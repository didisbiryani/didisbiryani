2. GOOGLE ANALYTICS 4 (GA4)
----------------------------------------------------
* Why is it needed?
Google Analytics is like a CCTV camera for your website. Without it, you have no idea who is visiting your site. It tells you:
- How many people visit didisbiryani.in every day.
- Where they are coming from (e.g., did they click a link on Google, WhatsApp, or Instagram?).
- What city they live in.
- Which biryani items they click on the most.
- How long they stay on your website before leaving.

3. META (FACEBOOK/INSTAGRAM) PIXEL
----------------------------------------------------
* Why is it needed?
If you ever plan to spend money on Facebook or Instagram ads to promote Didi's Biryani, the Meta Pixel is strictly mandatory. It tracks users crossing over from social media to your website. It allows you to do two highly profitable things:
- Tracking ROI: If you spend ₹500 on an Instagram ad, the Pixel tells you exactly how many actual Biryani orders came from that specific ₹500 ad.
- Retargeting: If a customer adds a Chicken Biryani to their cart but closes the website without paying, the Pixel remembers them. You can then show that exact person a Facebook ad the next day reminding them to complete their order.

* How to turn it on later:

3. Open your 'index.html' file in any code editor or notepad.
